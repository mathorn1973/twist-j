## The shape of the problem

standard Weil form W_J = W_xi from declared J-architecture as a manifestly
positive object (|A_J g|^2 or <g, H_J g>), without smuggling in zeta, its
zeros, the primes, or the form itself. Gates G0-G2 are accounting (closed
or freezable); G3-G4 are exactness of the arithmetic and archimedean sides;
G5 is the wall; G6 is the novelty condition. Every route below is scored
against G6 import risk and against what its FIRST finite falsifiable gate
would be.
