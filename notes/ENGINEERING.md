# Engineering witnesses (NON-CANONICAL)

Non canonical engineering record of the public series. Witnesses, not
theorem promotions; every number here is an engineering readout and
carries no claim weight. Items move here from the Canon under the
Genesis engineering disposition (data/ENGINEERING_DISPOSITION.tsv).

- `NGEHT-CORRIDOR`: the legacy readout was `mu >= 0.804` at three
  sigma with shadow excess in `[0, +0.93%]`. `SRC-EHT-M87` measures
  the M87* ring diameter; no public shadow-to-mu inference rule supports
  the corridor, so it is not a normative comparison.
- `NEUTRON-PPM`: the legacy bare-neutron comparison was reported as
  `-17.93 ppm`. The public source supplies the measured mass ratio, but
  no public `deg_v / chi` comparator or `Delta_EM` value reconstructs
  that residual.
- `RINGDOWN-WKB3`: the legacy minimal-exponential ringdown reading was
  reported as fired at `7.8 sigma` at WKB3 engineering grade. No public
  external source or reproducing test supports that grade.
- `CIRCUIT-COUNT`: random-circuit sampling was reported bit-exact in
  `Z[zeta_24]` and `Z[zeta_360]`; the native `Z_5` qudit suite was
  reported over 110000000 circuits with zero failures and three
  internal invariants in agreement.
- `SIX-PLATFORM-BITEXACT`: an integer Clifford+T statevector was
  reported bit-exact across six platforms.
- `HYDROGEN-HELIUM`: the internal chemistry bridge reported hydrogen
  to six significant figures and helium to 0.19 mHa.

The standing bar for a computation-only theorem is defined by the
public policy and activation gate, not by this note. Nothing in this
