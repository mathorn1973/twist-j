# Color measure selection (NON-CANONICAL DRAFT)

Post Genesis preregistration draft. Non canonical; no formal result,
no earned status, no run record exists or may exist during Genesis.

**Claim:** COLOR-MEASURE-SELECTION: a derived weight vector over the 24 carrier orbits, constrained by the 16 observable types, selects the color measure.

**Action layer:** L4

**Input object:** the public 24 orbit carrier and 16 type census of the color sector

**Observable:** the weight vector over the 24 orbits and its consistency with the 16 types

**PASS:** a public derivation selecting a unique admissible weight vector at the declared scope, two architectures

**FAIL:** a proof that symmetry plus the declared data admit no unique weight vector, recorded as a first class negative

**Budget:** one verifier bounded by the public check budget; any budget raise requires a disclosed amendment before computation

**Stop rule:** one pinned verifier per architecture; the orbit and type data are frozen at the pin

**Post-pin changes:** disclosed amendments only; no gate semantics,
threshold, or falsifier text may change after the pin; any such change
invalidates the run and requires a fresh candidate.
