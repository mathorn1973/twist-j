Post Genesis preregistration draft. Non canonical; no formal result,
no earned status, no run record exists or may exist during Genesis.

**Claim:** BELL-MAGIC-BOUNDARY: a precise CHSH functional with exact maxima over the zeta_5 and zeta_8 phase sets.

**Action layer:** L3

**Input object:** a declared CHSH functional on the two public phase sets, defined inside the verifier

**Observable:** the exact maxima over each phase set, closed form over Q(sqrt5) and Q(sqrt2)

**PASS:** both maxima computed in closed form and verified exactly on two architectures

**FAIL:** no closed form at the declared functional, or maxima contradicting the declared inequality structure

**Budget:** one verifier bounded by the public check budget; full enumeration of the finite phase sets only

**Stop rule:** one pinned verifier per architecture; the functional is frozen at the pin

**Post-pin changes:** disclosed amendments only; no gate semantics,
threshold, or falsifier text may change after the pin; any such change
invalidates the run and requires a fresh candidate.
