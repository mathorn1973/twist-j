## Audit ruling

The primitive Reynolds idea becomes finite and reviewable only after four
scope corrections:

1. the parent names ambient versus intrinsic commutators; the exterior term
   is a diagnostic residual, not a third curvature mode;
2. zero operators must remain in the family, since removing them could
   manufacture uniqueness;
3. the proposed equivalence is explicitly affine and selector-preserving;
   it is not asserted to be the full architecture automorphism group;
4. a non-canonical child family cannot positively close the parent claim.

The family below therefore has 640 raw syntactic specifications before
carrier routing, exact equality, or affine equivalence. The number 640 is an
upper bound on inventory rows, not a precomputed candidate or class count.

## 1. Carrier and uniform form

Freeze

```text

F = Q^X

(T_g f)(x) = f(g^(-1) x)

<f,h> = (1/|X|) sum_(x in X) f(x) h(x).
```

The five named public generators are ordered

```text
g_0=a < g_1=b < g_2=c < g_3=d < g_4=e.
```

They are affine involutive permutations of `X`. Hence every `T_g` is an
exact rational orthogonal involution and is self-adjoint for the uniform
form. Replacing the probability form by unnormalized counting multiplies the
form by one positive scalar and changes none of the adjoints or orthogonal
projectors below.

For every positive-dimensional V_S, Tr_S denotes the ordinary
endomorphism trace on End_Q(V_S). It is basis-independent and is not
divided by dim(V_S). The uniform inner product controls star; the
ordinary trace controls invariants such as Tr_S(K^2).

## 2. Reynolds projectors from exact orbit partitions

For every subset

```text
S subseteq {a,b,c,d,e}
```

let `Pi_S` be the canonical partition of `X` into connected components of
the generator graph with colors in `S`. Equivalently, these are the orbits
of the action generated by `S`, but the construction must not enumerate the
possibly large abstract subgroup.

Define the exact orbit-average projector

```text
(R_S f)(x)
  = (1/|O_S(x)|) sum_(y in O_S(x)) f(y),

P0 f
  = f - (1/|X|)(sum_(x in X) f(x)) 1,

P_S = P0 R_S = R_S P0,
V_S = image(P_S).
```

`R_S` and `P_S` are rational orthogonal projectors. Two subset
presentations are deduplicated only when their complete canonical orbit
partitions, and therefore their exact projectors, are equal. A hash may index
this comparison but is never an equality certificate.

Encode a checkpoint lexicographically by its base-5 integer and order the
orbits by their least encoded checkpoint. If the ordered partition is

```text
Pi_S = (O_0,...,O_(m-1)),
```

define the canonical mean-zero basis

```text
b_j = 1_(O_j) - (|O_j|/|O_(m-1)|) 1_(O_(m-1)),
B_S = (b_0,...,b_(m-2)).
```

Thus `dim(V_S)=m-1`. Every orbit-constant mean-zero function has one unique
coordinate vector in `B_S`; the exact Gram of `B_S` is computed from the
uniform inner product. Orbit indicators alone are not treated as coordinates
on `V_S`.

The child scope declares `dim(V_S)>0`. A raw row with `dim(V_S)=0` receives
the fixed route `DEGENERATE-CARRIER`; it is retained in the inventory and is
not silently deleted. This child-scope choice has no parent-status force.

## 3. Primitive ambient and intrinsic operators

For each of the ten canonically ordered pairs `u<v`, put

```text

K_AMB(S,u,v)
  = P_S C_uv P_S |_(V_S),

K_INT(S,u,v)
  = [P_S T_u P_S, P_S T_v P_S] |_(V_S).
```

The diagnostic exterior residual is computed but is not a candidate mode:

```text
K_EXT_DIAG(S,u,v)
  = P_S T_u (I-P_S) T_v P_S
    - P_S T_v (I-P_S) T_u P_S,

K_AMB = K_INT + K_EXT_DIAG.
```

The raw child inventory is therefore

Scale and orientation are fixed before classification:

Every exact operator is retained, including `K=0` on a positive-dimensional
carrier. `K_EXT_DIAG` has diagnostic provenance only and cannot create a
third representative.

## 4. Integrity theorems are not selection filters

For every well-formed positive-dimensional raw specification, the public
involutions and orthogonal projector imply

```text

```

These are construction-integrity checks. They may detect an implementation
error, but they are not data-dependent admissibility predicates and cannot

## 5. Explicit affine architecture equivalence

Define the proposal-local group `Aut_arch_aff` rather than an unqualified
`Aut_arch`. An element is an affine bijection

```text
h(x) = M x + t,
M in GL_6(F_5), t in F_5^6,
```

together with a permutation `pi` of the five generator labels, satisfying

```text
h g_i h^(-1) = g_(pi(i))                 for i=0,...,4,

pi_bar(z_6(x) + 2 epsilon mod 5)
  = z_6(h(x)) + 2 epsilon mod 5          for all x in X,
                                           epsilon in {0,1}.
```

Here `pi_bar` is the induced permutation of the numeric labels
`0,1,2,3,4`; the clock coordinate and Thue-Morse bit are fixed. This is the
exact covariance needed for the frozen selector. No claim is made that every
abstract automorphism of the architecture is affine.

Completeness for this chosen finite equivalence requires:

1. all 120 label permutations are visited;
2. the affine conjugacy and selector equations are solved exactly over
   `F_5`;
3. every free-parameter solution is exhausted exactly;
4. invertibility of `M` is decided exactly;
5. every retained `(M,t,pi)` is rechecked pointwise against the defining
   equations.

An exact RREF no-solution certificate is required for rejected label
permutations. A consistent linear system containing only singular matrices
requires a separate exact determinant/exhaustion certificate; consistency
alone is not an automorphism witness.

## 6. Operator equivalence and orientation cocycle

An affine architecture automorphism maps `Pi_S` to `Pi_(pi(S))` and induces
an exact rational intertwiner between `V_S` and `V_(pi(S))`. Canonical
reordering of the image pair contributes the fixed sign

```text
epsilon_pi(u,v) = +1  if pi(u)<pi(v),
                  -1  otherwise.
```

A candidate operator is the typed object

```text
TypedK = (canonical partition Pi_S, canonical basis B_S, matrix_[B_S](K)).
```

Exact equality before the affine quotient is permitted only when the
canonical carrier/partition is identical and the two rational matrices in
`B_S` are entrywise equal. In particular, zero maps on unequal carriers do
not collapse.

Two typed candidate rows are affine-equivalent only when a certified affine
architecture automorphism gives

```text
K' = epsilon_pi(u,v) T_h K T_h^(-1)
```

under the induced exact change from `B_S` to `B_(pi(S))`. The sign is not a
free quotient by `K -> -K`; it is solely the cocycle forced by canonical pair
orientation. The checker must verify its composition law and transitivity.

Mode labels are provenance, not invariants. `AMB` and `INT` rows on the same
typed carrier that produce the same rational matrix are collapsed by exact
equality before the affine quotient.

1. constructs all 640 raw rows in the fixed order;
2. routes `dim(V_S)=0` rows to `DEGENERATE-CARRIER`;
3. forms `TypedK` for every admissible row;
4. collapses exact equality only within an identical typed carrier, choosing

## 8. Certificate schema and checker

For each of the 640 raw specifications, the certificate contains:

```text
raw tuple id
subset and complete canonical orbit partition
ordered orbit sizes and canonical basis pivot
exact basis Gram
carrier route and dim(V_S)
ordered primitive pair and candidate mode
exact operator expression tag and sparse primitive references
zero/nonzero flag without filtering
exact-equality representative and typed equality witness
affine-equivalence representative and witness, if any
final child disposition
```

The operator expression is reconstructed from five exact permutation arrays,
the orbit partition, `P0`, and the `AMB` or `INT` expression. A cached matrix
is optional. The checker applies the expression to every vector of `B_S`,
converts the result through the unique canonical mean-zero coordinates, and
compares rational columns exactly. Dense `|X|`-square matrices and hashes are
never accepted as sole equality evidence.

For affine equivalence, each of the 120 label permutations yields a linear
system over `F_5` in the 42 entries of `(M,t)`. Exact RREF gives a
free-parameter dimension `d_pi<=42`. The completeness fallback enumerates
all `5^d_pi` assignments, decides `det(M)!=0`, and rechecks the generator and
selector equations pointwise. Thus a finite worst-case bound is

```text
120 * 5^42 affine assignments before linear pruning.
```

This bound proves decidability but not practical run cost. The companion
`P-CURVATURE-OPERATOR-CANONICAL-1-AUT-REDUCTION.md` supplies a
proposal-local exact reduction: selector covariance leaves five cyclic
label shifts, four have inconsistent affine systems, and the identity shift
has the unique solution `M=I,t=0`. Thus the fallback is not reached on this
declared surface. A future public checker must still implement and certify
that reduction; a sampled, floating-point, or unreviewed private search is
`STOP`.

The certificate checker independently:

1. rebuilds `X`, the five generator permutations, all 32 canonical
   partitions, all bases `B_S`, and all 640 raw tuple IDs;
2. recomputes every carrier route, operator action, ordinary trace, and typed
   exact-equality representative;
3. exhausts the affine solver by the declared exact method;
4. verifies every intertwiner, orientation sign, and cocycle composition;
5. rebuilds the equivalence graph and its connected components;
6. verifies the raw-to-representative surjection and that no two final
   representatives remain equivalent.

Any missing row, unresolved coordinate conversion, unverified collision, or
incomplete affine route produces `STOP`.
