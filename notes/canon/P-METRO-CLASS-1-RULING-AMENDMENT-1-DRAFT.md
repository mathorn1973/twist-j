## 1. Why an amendment is forced either way

The controlling ruling requires two independently defined maps,
`Adm_direct` and `Adm_compositional` (from the separately defined factor
maps), and routes their disagreement as `NEGATIVE`. The merged fork
witness proves, by an exact two-state member of the class, that the
factorwise compositional decision diverges where the direct anchored
decision exists. Consequence, stated plainly: with the factorwise reading
of `Adm_compositional`, the `NEGATIVE` clause "the direct and
compositional predicates disagree" is already decided by a sealed finite
theorem before any probe runs. A frozen routing whose negative headline
is pre-decided cannot carry a blind scientific outcome. The owner must
therefore either amend the compositional channel or knowingly accept a
pre-decided `NEGATIVE`.

## 2. The two amendment options, typed

```text
OPTION JOINT-CERTIFICATE (recommended)
  Replace the factorwise reading of Adm_compositional by
  Adm_certificate: the joint simultaneous primary/peripheral spectral
  decomposition of the commuting factor matrices, restricted to the
  reachable submodule generated by the allowed starts and the readout,
  with exact certificates for peripheral phases, Jordan structure, and
  terminal sectors. Adm_certificate is defined by its own algebra with no
  reference to Adm_direct; the target theorem
      Adm_direct(P) = Adm_certificate(P)
  stays the probe content and the equality still may not be used as the
  definition of either side (the sealed one-dimensional precedent is an
  iff of exactly this shape).
  Routing changes:
      NEGATIVE keeps its other clauses unchanged (normalization not
      total; two reduction-equivalent protocols receiving different
      decisions; two required factorizations disagreeing), all of which
      remain genuinely open;
      direct-versus-certificate disagreement is retyped STOP (integrity),
      never a scientific negative;
      the factorwise non-equivalence is registered as a computed control,
      CONTROL-FACTORWISE-NONEQUIV, carrying the merged witness, so the
      factorwise law cannot be silently reintroduced later.

OPTION INDEPENDENT-FACTORWISE (status quo made explicit)
  Keep Adm_compositional factorwise. Then the ruling must record that the
  disagreement clause of NEGATIVE is pre-decided by the merged witness,
  the probe headline re-derives a sealed non-equivalence, and every later
  protocol in the class inherits a permanent disagreement channel that is
  a property of the definitions, not of physics. This option is coherent
  but spends the probe on a known theorem.
```

## 3. Recommendation

```text
RECOMMEND: OPTION JOINT-CERTIFICATE.
```

Grounds: the sealed one-dimensional layer already types the spectral side
as the decidable characterization of the direct property, not as a second
science channel; the witness shows the factorwise alternative is not
equivalent already at the smallest nontrivial size; and the amendment
preserves every genuinely open negative clause while moving the
pre-decided one into a named control. The non-circularity requirement of
the controlling ruling is preserved verbatim: two independently defined
maps, equality as target, never as definition.
