# Canon changelog (public series)

## Public Canon v1 (candidate)

Genesis entry. Initial public synthesis: the thematic Canon in
nineteen sections; 44 registered claims (11 theorem grade, of which 8
carry minimal reproductions and 3 carry inline derivations; 4 computed
grade, the kernel census cluster, carried by a full enumeration
witness with closure and stability checks; 29 live frontier rows, each
with a concrete falsifier or a decision condition stating what closes
it positively and what closes it negatively); four reproduction
witnesses (kernel, alpha-exact-lemma, born-faces, census); the core,
the frontier, the registry, and the citation record. The public series
starts here; development before this document is outside the normative
series and is referenced only by the cutover audit.
